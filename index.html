2010 US Population Density by County

This map displays the population density of US counties in 2010.

This map was generated utilizing QGIS 2.14.4-Essen digital mapping software.

Population density was calculated by normalizing county population by the area of the county in square miles.

  • See the map:2010 US Population Density by County
  • Data Sources: 2010 US Census Bureau
